Jordle

Information

This game is a Wordle variant, made for my girlfriend Jorien. It varies slightly from the base game, since valid guesses and solutions are all 6-letter Dutch words.

Same rules as in the base game apply:

  • Green: in the word & correct spot
  • Yellow: in the word, but wrong spot
  • Grey: not in the word
